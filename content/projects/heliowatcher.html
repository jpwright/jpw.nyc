--- title: Heliowatcher ---

March 2012 — May 2012

The HelioWatcher is a solar tracking & analytics platform, capable of orientating a solar panel towards the position of maximum efficiency and performing long-term data logging to analyze optimal positioning. Developed with Jeremy Blum for Cornell's ECE 4760.
